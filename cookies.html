We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored in your browser to help us:

  • Understand how people use our site
  • Improve performance
  • Process payments (via Stripe)

What Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: for basic site functionality
  • Analytics cookies: to help us improve the user experience
  • Payment cookies: required by third-party processors like Stripe

Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser. This may affect site performance.
